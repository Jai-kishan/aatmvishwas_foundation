About Our Charity

We, a group of education and social enthusiasts, came together to start a learning center in Amagachi village of Arariya district in Bihar, which area is highly deprived of education, health, and development. Vijay Paswan, who belongs to Amagachi, started the learning center in October 2022 with limited resourse and strongest desire to serve community.

Currently, our initiative helps around 50-60 kids,Â many of whom do not have formal school access. It is in early stage right now registeration process is in progress.

All the team members who are working for this initiative are volunteers but also working full-time job to support themselves and their families. It takes a pipeline of organisations to let problem solvers live up to their fullest potential.
